Meet the owner

Hi, I'm Dan — the electrician behind Brightwire

After more than a decade wiring everything from new builds to century-old homes, I started Brightwire Electric to do things my way: take the time to do it right, explain the work in plain language, and never leave a mess behind.

As a licensed master electrician, I handle the permits, follow code to the letter, and stand behind every job. Whether it's a flickering light or a full panel upgrade, you'll deal with me directly — not a rotating cast of subcontractors.

When I'm not on a job, you'll find me coaching my kid's soccer team and tinkering with anything that has a circuit board.
